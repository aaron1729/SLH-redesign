Who and How We Serve

A program for women-led families experiencing homelessness who need more time and support than emergency shelters can provide.

Our unique role is not only that we help single mothers and their children experiencing homelessness, but that we specifically help those women-led families that need more time and support than emergency shelters can provide, but who do not meet the requirements for other housing programs.

Through deeply affordable housing and wraparound services, families receive individualized support that fosters education, financial literacy, strong family relationships, and healthy living, leading to lifetime self-sufficiency.

For families who do not qualify for Saint Louise House housing, we provide referrals to partner agencies in the Austin community.
