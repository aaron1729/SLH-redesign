Our Mission

Empowering women and children from homelessness to healthy independent lives

Saint Louise House is a nonprofit organization dedicated to the success of women and their children moving from homelessness to healthy independent lives. We strive to empower women and their children to overcome homelessness for generations to come. Our proven program provides stable housing and wraparound services with highly-individualized support that fosters education, financial literacy, strong family relationships and healthy living, leading to a lifetime of self-sufficiency.

Our Program

A two-generational wraparound model

Families work with Empowerment Partners and receive support in stable housing, education, financial literacy, family relationships, and healthy living.

1

Stable Housing

Deeply affordable, fully furnished apartments give families the security needed to focus on goals and growth.

2

Empowerment Partners

Licensed social workers provide individualized weekly support and resource coordination.

3

Life Skills and Child Development

Curriculum-based financial literacy and parenting support plus tutoring and social-emotional activities for children.
