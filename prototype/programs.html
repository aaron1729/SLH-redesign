Services

Our highly-individualized support model helps women and children move from homelessness to healthy independent lives.

Our Program

Two-generational wraparound support

Families at Saint Louise House can expect to receive highly-individualized support from licensed social workers and program staff to ensure all needs are met. We utilize a two-generational approach with support for both mother and child to break the cycle of poverty and homelessness for generations to come.

Each family receives support across the following core service areas:

  • Stable Housing: deeply affordable and fully furnished apartments.
  • Education: individualized education and workforce advancement support.
  • Financial Literacy: budgeting and planning using Your Money, Your Goals.
  • Family Relationships: parenting coaching using Nurturing Parenting.
  • Healthy Living: social-emotional support, food, transportation, and basic needs.
