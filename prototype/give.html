Donation process and wish list

How to donate items

  • Email photos of furniture to donations@saintlouisehouse.org for approval.
  • Call 512.302.0027 to schedule a drop-off date and time.
  • No pickup is available; all donations must be delivered.

Current wish list

  • Toiletries and soap
  • Laundry detergent
  • Cleaning products
  • Pots, pans, dishes, and utensils
  • Food pantry staples
