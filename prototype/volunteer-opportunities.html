Volunteer Opportunities

Support Saint Louise House by volunteering your time and skills.

Volunteer Roles

Ways to support families

Monthly Birthday Bakes

Make every mother and child feel special on their birthday by providing a personalized cake.

Family Dinners

Support family participation in workshops by providing a meal for up to 25 people to share together beforehand.

Product Drives

Coordinate a drive for toiletries, laundry detergent, and cleaning supplies for our on-site pantry.

Seasonal Projects and Events

Support holiday drives, back-to-school preparation, and special Saint Louise House events.

Administrative Support

Assist with office projects, mailings, and event preparation when opportunities are available.

Translation

Help us meet families where they are by providing Spanish translation for documents and applications.
