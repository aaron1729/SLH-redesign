Overview

Saint Louise House at a glance

Saint Louise House was founded in 2000 by Saint Austin Catholic Parish and the Daughters of Charity. For 25 years, we have provided stable housing and highly-individualized wraparound support for women-led families experiencing homelessness in Austin.

Our mission is to empower women and their children to overcome homelessness for generations to come through long-term supportive housing and services that advance education, financial literacy, family stability, and healthy living.
