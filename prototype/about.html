Our Journey

Our history

2000 -- Founded in Austin

Saint Louise House was established to fill a critical gap in services for single mothers and their children experiencing homelessness in Austin, Texas.

Today -- Over Two Decades of Impact

For more than 25 years, Saint Louise House has provided single mothers and their children the stable housing and two-generational wraparound services they need to move from homelessness to healthy, independent lives.

Our People

Leadership

Our dedicated leaders bring decades of experience in social services, nonprofit management, and community development.

LW

Laura Ward

Executive Director

Laura began her career in 1994 advocating for the needs of families and children as a licensed attorney at Victim Services in NYC. She went on to serve as Executive Director of Foster Care Review in Florida, then as General Counsel for the Travis County Juvenile Probation Department and Division Director for the Office of Children's Services. She holds a J.D. from George Washington University National Law Center and a B.A. in psychology from the University of Michigan.
