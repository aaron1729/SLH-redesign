In-Kind Donations

Support families with essential items

How to donate

  • Email photos of furniture to donations@saintlouisehouse.org for approval.
  • Call 512.302.0027 to schedule drop-off.
  • No pick-ups are available; donations must be delivered.

Most-needed items

  • Toiletries
  • Laundry detergent
  • Cleaning supplies
  • Household essentials
  • Approved furniture in good condition

Questions

Contact our donations team for current needs, accepted items, and scheduling details.
