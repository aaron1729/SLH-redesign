In-Kind Donations

Wish List

These are items our families need most. Please review the drop-off instructions below before donating.

Furniture

  • Living room chairs
  • Side tables
  • Coffee tables
  • TV stands (no glass tops)
  • Dressers without mirrors
  • Chest of drawers
  • 4-person dining tables and chairs

Kitchen & Household

  • Cookie sheets
  • Pot holders
  • Can openers
  • Vegetable peelers
  • Toasters
  • Small and tall trash cans
  • Swiffers

How to Donate Items

  • Furniture: Email photos to donations@saintlouisehouse.org for approval before bringing items
  • Schedule drop-off: Call 512.302.0027 to arrange a delivery time
  • No pickups available -- all donations must be delivered to our location

Items We Cannot Accept

Due to space and safety considerations, we are unable to accept the following items:

Sofas · Recliners · Mattresses · Box springs · Mattress covers · Full/queen/king beds and/or bedding · Used linens · Used towels or washcloths · Cribs · Toddler beds · Toddler sheets · Baby highchairs · Baby furniture · Car seats · Used baby bottles · Used sippy cups · Used plastic tumblers from restaurants · Clothing · Toys · Glass-topped furniture · Candles
