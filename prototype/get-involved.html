Volunteer with Saint Louise House

Our volunteers are essential to the work we do. Whether you have a few hours a month or want to commit to an ongoing role, there is a place for you.

Get Started

Provide Meals

Prepare and deliver meals for up to 25 people before 4:30 p.m. to support life skills workshops. Volunteers may serve and assist with cleanup.

Birthday Cakes

Bake or purchase a birthday cake for a resident’s child. Every child deserves to feel celebrated on their special day. Drop off during business hours.

Translation Assistance

Help translate documents from English into Spanish, enter application information into our database, or provide interpretation services as needed.

Welcome Home Team

Help furnish and set up apartments for new families moving in. Teams organize donated furniture, household items, and personal touches so families arrive to a home that’s ready.

Food Bank Delivery

Pick up orders from the Central Texas Food Bank and deliver to Saint Louise House. This monthly commitment takes about 1–2 hours.

Community Nights

Join our regular Community Nights events where volunteers and families come together for dinner, activities, and fellowship on the Saint Louise House campus.

In-kind Donations

Wish list & donation guidelines

Furniture

  • Living room chairs
  • Side tables
  • Coffee tables
  • TV stands (no glass tops)
  • Dressers without mirrors
  • Chest of drawers
  • 4-person dining tables and chairs

Kitchen & Household

  • Cookie sheets
  • Pot holders
  • Can openers
  • Vegetable peelers
  • Toasters
  • Small and tall trash cans
  • Swiffers

How to Donate Items

  • Furniture: Email photos to donations@saintlouisehouse.org for approval before bringing items
  • Schedule drop-off: Call 512.302.0027 to arrange a delivery time
  • No pickups available -- all donations must be delivered to our location

Items We Cannot Accept

Due to space and safety considerations, we are unable to accept the following items:

Sofas · Reclining chairs · Mattresses · Bed linens · Towels · Toddler beds · Cribs · Baby furniture · Car seats · Baby bottles · Sippy cups · Plastic tumblers · Clothing · Toys · Glass-topped furniture · Candles
